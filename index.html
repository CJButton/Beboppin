Beboppin' is a 2D scrolling game based on the hit show Cowboy Bebop. It's 2071 and you are a space bounty hunter, a Space Cowboy.

You'll pilot the 'Swordfish II' as you chase after your bounty. Unfortunately, they've just dashed into an asteroid field. Go in after them Cowboy!

Avoid the space rocks! Your controls are:

A / ← : Left            D / → : Right

W / ↑ : Up           S / ↓ : Down

M : Mute/ Unmute       P : Pause/ Unpause
